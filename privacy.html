Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 10, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to CharadesCam. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe user experience.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

CharadesCam is designed to work entirely on your device. We do not collect or store any personal information on our servers. Any data you generate through the app remains on your device.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

We do not automatically collect any personal data, usage statistics, or analytics. The app functions entirely offline without sending data to external servers.

2.3 Camera and Microphone Access

CharadesCam requests access to your device's camera and microphone solely for the purpose of recording your charades gameplay. All recordings are:

  • Stored locally on your device
  • Never uploaded to our servers or third parties
  • Completely under your control to delete or share
  • Only created when you explicitly enable the recording feature

2.4 Media Library Access

We request permission to save videos to your device's photo library. This permission is only used when you choose to save a video you've recorded. We never access or read other media from your library.

3. How We Use Your Information

Since we don't collect personal information, there is no data to use, share, or sell. The app operates entirely on your device:

  • Game settings are stored locally using your device's storage
  • Video recordings remain on your device unless you choose to share them
  • No gameplay data, statistics, or user behavior is transmitted or analyzed

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to third parties because we don't collect it. CharadesCam does not integrate with any analytics services, advertising networks, or third-party SDKs that collect user data.

When you choose to share videos using your device's native sharing functionality, you are directly using your device's operating system features. We are not involved in that sharing process.

5. Data Security

All your data remains on your device and is protected by your device's security measures. We recommend:

  • Keeping your device's operating system updated
  • Using device encryption and screen lock features
  • Being mindful of what you record and share

6. Children's Privacy

CharadesCam is suitable for all ages. Since we don't collect any personal information from any users, including children under 13, we comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Parents and guardians should supervise children when using video recording features and sharing functionality.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

  • Camera/Microphone Permissions: You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device settings
  • Recorded Videos: You can delete videos from your device's photo library at any time
  • App Data: You can clear all app data by uninstalling CharadesCam
  • Recording Control: Recording is always optional and can be disabled in settings

8. International Users

CharadesCam works entirely on your device regardless of your location. Since we don't collect or transfer data, there are no international data transfer concerns.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Providing an in-app notification for significant changes

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

Email: privacy@charadescam.app

Summary

In short: CharadesCam respects your privacy by not collecting any of your personal data. Everything happens on your device, and you have full control over any videos you create. We believe games should be fun without compromising your privacy.
